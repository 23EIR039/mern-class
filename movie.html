The Conjuring
Based on a true story, The Conjuring follows paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren as they help a family terrorized by a dark presence in their farmhouse. The chilling encounters escalate, revealing a powerful demonic force rooted in the house's disturbing history. The Warrens uncover a history of witchcraft, curses, and haunted spirits, fighting to contain the evil before it consumes the family entirely. A suspenseful and terrifying tale of supernatural evil.
